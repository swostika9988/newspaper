About Us

Human service is the best way of life

Chitwan Manav Sewa Ashram was established in 2072 B.S. (2015 A.D.) with a slogan "Human service is the best way of life". We are located at Bharatpur Chitwan. Chitwan Manav Sewa Ashram is taking care of oldage and differently-abled people. Every person is equal and have equal right. So, we started this organisation for taking care of needy ones.
